Good ol' Cereal

Cereal is a pillar of the food community. From children's breakfast, a teenager's snack, to an adults depression meal at 3am because they've lost control of their lives and all that can comfort them is a cold bowl of whatever cereal they remembered to buy the last time they went shopping. Here's a recipe to soothe that anguish or have a hardy breakfast in the morning!

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of desired size
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • Cereal of your choice!
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. place desired amount of cereal within the bowl
  2. pour 2 cups of milk into bowl (more/less if desired)
  3. insert spoon into cereal laiden bowl
  4. Begin consuming
  5. Forget your worries
