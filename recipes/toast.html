Toast

This Is Toast

This is toast. As a young boy I ate many slices of sad, uncooked, floppy bread. With this recipe I will show you how to make Toast, the superior form, of bread in all of its glory. It is a simple, yet very delicate process, but with my help you'll make the toast almost right every time!

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Butter
  • Enthusiasm to learn
  • Suspension of disbelief

Steps

  1. Grab slices of bread
  2. insert bread into toaster
  3. set toaster timer to desired darkness
  4. wait the excruciatingly long time it takes to toast bread
  5. Be surprised when it pops up
  6. put literally as much butter as you want upon your toast
  7. enjoy
