Milk time

Milk is the life blood of any child growing up, teenagers that just want something different, and adults, who are mixing it with alcohol. In this recipe I will instruct you on how to make a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon (or half gallon or pint) of Milk
  • 1 drinking cup
  • Towel for messes

Steps

  1. Prepare your cup to drink out of
  2. Remove jug of milk from its cage
  3. unscrew the lid of the milk
  4. With extreme precision, pour the milk into the glass
  5. With the towel clean up any spilt milk so you don't have to cry about it
  6. enjoy
